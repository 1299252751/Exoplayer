implements org.robolectric.shadows.ShadowMediaCodec.CodecConfig.Codec, Dumper.Dumpable
    A ShadowMediaCodec.CodecConfig.Codec for Robolectric's ShadowMediaCodec that records the contents of buffers passed to it before copying the contents into the output buffer.

    This also implements Dumper.Dumpable so the recorded buffers can be written out to a dump file.
